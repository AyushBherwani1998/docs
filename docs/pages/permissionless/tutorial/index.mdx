# Tutorials

Learning-oriented lessons that take you through a series of steps to complete a project. Most useful when you want to get started with Pimlico.

[Tutorial 1](/permissionless/tutorial/tutorial-1) takes you through the journey of signing up to Pimlico, and leveraging permissionless.js's high-level APIs to easily create and bundle a user operation.
[Tutorial 2](/permissionless/tutorial/tutorial-2) takes you through the journey of signing up to Pimlico, creating a user operation, getting it sponsored by our verifying paymaster, and then sending it to be included on-chain with our bundler.
[Tutorial 3](/permissionless/tutorial/tutorial-3) takes you through the journey of sponsoring your first user operation with USDC with Pimlico's ERC-20 paymaster.
